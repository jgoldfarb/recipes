Ingredients:
  - 6 eggs
  - butter
  - milk
  - smoked lox

Directions:
  1. Crack eggs into a bowl.

  2. Add a little milk.

  3. Add a few pieces of freshly cut lox

  4. Stir mixture together with a fork until eggs are an even yellow consistency.

  5. Heat saucepan over medium flame.

  6. Add a teaspoon of butter to grease the pan.

  7. When saucepan is hot, pour egg mixture into saucepan.

  8. Stir eggs frequently until fully cooked.

  9. Serve.
